The algorithm for dynamic stochastic synthesis is presented by Iannis Xenakis in his book "Formalized Music" [1]. Additional ideas were taken from [2] and [3].

[1] I. Xenakis and S. Kanach, Formalized Music, vol. 17, no. 2. 1993.
[2] M.-H. Serra, “Stochastic Composition and Stochastic Timbre: GENDY3 by Iannis Xenakis,” Perspect. New Music, vol. 31, no. 1, p. 236, 1993.
[3] S. Luque, “The Stochastic Synthesis of Iannis Xenakis,” Leonardo Music J., vol. 19, pp. 77–84, 2009.
